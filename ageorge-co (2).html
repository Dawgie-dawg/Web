What We Do

Mergers & Acquisitions

AGeorge & Co. provides strategic, financial, and tactical advice in connection with executing complex mergers, acquisitions, company sales, and corporate divestitures — including carve-outs, joint ventures, spin-offs, and takeover preparedness.

We serve both buyers and sellers across all transaction structures. Our principals have deep experience navigating competitive processes, cross-border complexities, and sensitive negotiation dynamics.

What this covers

  • Sell-side processes & negotiated sales
  • Buy-side search & acquisition advisory
  • Mergers & business combinations
  • Carve-outs, spin-offs & divestitures
  • Joint ventures & strategic partnerships
  • Takeover defence & preparedness

Capital Advisory

We advise clients on the full spectrum of capital raising alternatives — including debt, equity, and hybrid capital — and help identify alternative sources of growth capital such as private credit and sovereign investors.

Our independence means we are never conflicted by balance sheet relationships. Our sole objective is the best capital structure outcome for our clients.

What this covers

  • Growth equity & minority capital raises
  • Debt financing & refinancing advisory
  • Hybrid & convertible structures
  • Pre-IPO capital & liquidity solutions
  • Investor identification & syndication
  • Recapitalisations & balance sheet optimisation

Restructuring & Liability Management

We provide comprehensive financial restructuring and capital structure advice for stressed and distressed situations — including out-of-court solutions, in-court processes, and liability management transactions.

What this covers

  • Debtor-side restructuring advisory
  • Creditor-side & ad hoc group advisory
  • Distressed M&A & asset sales
  • Debt exchanges & amendments
  • Chapter 11 & cross-border insolvency
  • Balance sheet recapitalisations

Special Committee Advisory

AGeorge & Co. provides strategic, financial, and process advice to directors and other fiduciaries navigating transactions with actual, apparent, or potential conflicts of interest.

What this covers

  • Going-private & take-private transactions
  • Related-party & conflicted transactions
  • Fairness opinions & financial analyses
  • Minority squeeze-outs & buyouts
  • Fiduciary process management
  • Litigation support & expert testimony

Shareholder Engagement

We advise management teams and boards on shareholder votes, proxy matters, and activist situations — helping clients navigate the shareholder landscape with clarity and conviction.

What this covers

  • Activist preparedness & vulnerability analysis
  • Activist defence campaign management
  • Proxy contest advisory
  • Shareholder vote strategy
  • Investor relations strategy
  • Board governance advisory

Private Funds Advisory

We provide liquidity solutions to alternative asset managers and their limited partners across private equity, private credit, infrastructure, venture, and real estate — with deep expertise in GP-led secondaries.

What this covers

  • GP-led secondary transactions
  • Fund continuation vehicles
  • LP portfolio solutions
  • GP advisory & strategic alternatives
  • Fund restructurings
  • NAV financing advisory
