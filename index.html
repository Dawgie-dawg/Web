Our Team

The people
behind the work.

Alex George
Managing Director

Alex George is the founder and Managing Director of A. George & Co., a boutique M&A advisory firm serving the middle market. He began his career advising institutional clients at the World Bank IFC and Point 72 Asset Management before joining Carriage Hill, a middle market M&A firm, where he developed his transactional practice. Mr. George holds a degree from Ohio University, where he graduated Magna Cum Laude.

Sean Burr
Senior Advisor

Sean Burr is a Senior Advisor at A. George & Co. and a serial entrepreneur with multiple successful exits. He currently serves as CEO of Solar Slice, Principal at Inverted Electric, and board member of Pioneer Solar, having built and scaled businesses across the energy, construction, and technology sectors. Mr. Burr holds a B.S. from Prescott College.
