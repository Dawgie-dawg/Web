About AGeorge & Co.

Selective by Design.
Committed to Results.

AGeorge & Co. is a specialized M&A advisory practice focused exclusively on the facility management industry — janitorial, soft services, hard services, and building services businesses across the middle market.

We work with a limited number of clients at any time. That selectivity is intentional — it is how we ensure that every engagement receives the full strategic attention, deep sector knowledge, and complete discretion it deserves. Transactions are never discussed until they are done.
