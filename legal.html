Privacy Policy
Data
AGeorge & Co. is committed to protecting the privacy of the principals, business owners, and counterparties with whom we work. This policy describes our practices regarding information collected through this website and in connection with our advisory engagements.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal and business information that you voluntarily provide when contacting us, requesting information, or entering into a formal engagement. This may include name, company name, title, contact details, financial information relevant to an advisory matter, and other details shared in the ordinary course of a transaction or inquiry.

When you visit this website, we may automatically receive standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs. This information is used solely for website performance and security purposes and is not linked to any personally identifiable profile.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and assess potential advisory engagements
  • To perform M&A advisory, due diligence facilitation, and related services under a signed engagement agreement
  • To comply with applicable legal, regulatory, and professional obligations
  • To maintain and improve the functionality of this website

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties. Information may be shared with counterparties, professional advisors (legal counsel, accountants, lenders), or service providers strictly on a need-to-know basis and under obligations of confidentiality. We may disclose information as required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.

Cookies & Tracking

This website may use minimal cookies for session management and analytics. We do not deploy advertising trackers or third-party remarketing pixels. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; this will not materially affect your ability to access or use this website.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, to comply with applicable legal obligations, and to resolve disputes or enforce agreements. Information related to formal advisory engagements is typically retained for seven years following conclusion of the matter.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable law and any overriding professional obligations. Requests should be directed to the contact information below.

Regulatory Disclosures
Compliance
Nothing on this website constitutes an offer to sell or a solicitation of an offer to buy any security. AGeorge & Co. does not act as a broker-dealer and is not registered as such with FINRA or the SEC.

Nature of Services

AGeorge & Co. LLC provides mergers and acquisitions advisory services exclusively to principals — owners, founders, and management teams — in the facility management and services sector. Our engagements are private, bespoke, and conducted under written advisory agreements.

No Securities Brokerage

AGeorge & Co. does not act as a broker-dealer, placement agent, or intermediary in the purchase or sale of securities as defined under U.S. federal or state law. Our advisory activities are structured and conducted in accordance with applicable exemptions. Where a transaction involves securities, appropriate registered broker-dealer services will be engaged or referred as required by law.

No Investment Advice

Nothing contained on this website or in any communication from AGeorge & Co. constitutes investment advice, financial advice, or a recommendation to buy or sell any asset, security, or business interest. All information provided is for general informational purposes only. Prospective clients should obtain independent legal, financial, and tax counsel before entering any transaction.

Past Performance

Any references to prior transactions, deal outcomes, or sector experience are provided for illustrative purposes only. Past advisory outcomes are not indicative of future results. Each transaction is unique and subject to its own risks, market conditions, and commercial circumstances.

Jurisdictional Matters

AGeorge & Co. LLC is organised in the State of New York. Our advisory services are principally offered within the United States. Nothing on this website constitutes an offer or solicitation in any jurisdiction where such activities would be unlawful.

Conflicts of Interest

We disclose to clients, prior to engagement, any material conflicts of interest known at the time of engagement. Our principal obligation is to the client we are engaged to represent. We do not represent both buyer and seller in the same transaction without full written disclosure and informed consent from all relevant parties.

Confidentiality
Engagement
Discretion is foundational to our practice. We work exclusively with principals and treat every inquiry, engagement, and transaction with strict confidence.

Our Commitment

AGeorge & Co. treats all information received from clients, counterparties, and prospective clients as strictly confidential. We do not disclose the existence or details of any advisory engagement, transaction, or inquiry without the express consent of the relevant principal.

All formal advisory mandates are governed by a written engagement agreement that includes comprehensive confidentiality obligations consistent with professional M&A advisory practice. These obligations survive the conclusion of any engagement.
